Study Focus

The University of Washington MS degree provided advanced training in geospatial technologies. The course provided advanced training in web mapping development in various JS libraries to include Leaflet, ArcGIS, and Google Maps API. We utilized the IOS Mapkit framework to display and create specific IOS mobile apps for editing, geolocation, and routing services. We utilized industry software, hardware and open source software to develop our own specific location-based services, location-based data analysis and visualization techniques. My capstone thesis and app were based on the utilization of GIS during emergency management and integrating social media for shared situational awareness during a response.
